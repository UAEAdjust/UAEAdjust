UAE ADJUST

This is the homepage for the website known as UAE ADJUST, this website is dedicated to helping expats to adjust to the UAE with its social and cultural differences to most countries around the world. Here you can find many different webpages that will go over different parts of the process to adjust to the UAE, such as cultural differences, activities you can do in the country and a Q&A section.

About myself

My name is Timur Dzhanan, I am 14 years old and I have been living in Dubai for the past 3 years, and I have made UAEAdjust to help people that are moving to Dubai, or planning to move to dubai, with adjusting to the social and cultural changes that one might face when they come to Dubai.
